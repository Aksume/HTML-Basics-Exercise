Spaghetti Alfredo.

Ingredients

  • 1.large egg
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh basil (plus more for serving)
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

    1. Cook Spaghetti: Cook the spaghetti according to the directions on the package. Do not drain it yet.
    2. Heat Oil: In the meantime, heat olive oil in a large skillet set over medium heat.
    3. Add Garlic: Add garlic to the oil and cook for 30 seconds, or until fragrant. Do not burn the garlic.
    4. Whisk: Whisk the cream cheese and water until well combined.
    5. Add Cheese & Seasonings: Whisk in the parmesan cheese; taste for salt and pepper and adjust accordingly. sauce consistency.
    6. Drain Spaghetti: Drain your spaghetti and then return it back to the cooking pot.
    7. Add Sauce: Add the cream cheese sauce to the spaghetti; stir until combined.
    8. Garnish & Serve: Garnish with a crack of black pepper and chopped parsley, if desired.
